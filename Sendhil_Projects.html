Image Manipulation

The Image Manipulation Project, was a task created to test our skills and understanding in manipulating images through python. In this project we were split up into duo's in which I was paired up with Yash. Given the freedom of creativity, we decided to continue our project focused on designing memes for the Dublin Teacher's Association. We spent two weeks working together and constantly improcing our code to fit our goals. We tweaked our initial designs as what we envisioned clearly was to far out of reach for our skills. Evenetually we settled on creating a meme creator that had a user interface in the command line, that could create memes through either templates, or even to create your own.
