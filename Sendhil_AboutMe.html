"One To Strive To Excellence Through Efforts"

This concept was drilled even harder into my head, through the numerous years of martial arts. “One to strive for excellence through efforts”. This quote plays a big part in the reason that I am driven, as I always want to demonstrate to people that I am dedicated to a cause. To me, there is no better way to being dedicated to a cause than to be driven. Though I am very driven, I will never put forth the idea of cheating or finding shortcuts to produce, as it goes against some of my most basic morals.


As mentioned above I also work extremely well with people in a team. This is solely because of my multiple years of being in various sports. I have used these skills in various group projects, where I have led others by example, such as the CAP project. This CAP project was a project to bring social awareness about the litter found across our town. My group and I worked together for over one month, giving me plenty of opportunity to showcase my teamwork and leadership skills.


Overall, using my skills that I have gained over the years I would like to find a applicable job that will keep me happy. I am nowhere near to mastering anything, but there is a road ahead.
